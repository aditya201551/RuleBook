Game Objectives

The Spy

Other Players

Gameplay Flow

  1. Round Length: 6-10 minutes (shorter for smaller groups, longer for larger)
  2. Location Setup: Each player receives a location card. All players except the spy see the same location (e.g., "The Bank"). The spy receives a "Spy" card and doesn't know the location.
  3. Questioning: The game leader starts by asking another player a question about the location (e.g., "Is this place where children are welcome?")
  4. Answering: The questioned player must answer. No follow-up questions allowed. After answering, they ask someone else a question.
  5. No Retaliation Questions: You cannot immediately question someone who just questioned you.
